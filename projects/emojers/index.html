emojer

Physical hardware devices that send emoji messages between two people, no matter the distance. Select an emoji on one device and it appears on the other

A pair of physical devices that let you send emoji messages to someone you care about. When you select an emoji on one device, it appears on the other - no matter how far apart you are. Built around the concept that the most meaningful digital communication often happens with the simplest gestures. Sometimes a single emoji says more than a paragraph.
